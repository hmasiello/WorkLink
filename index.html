WorkLink is an employment resource that assists businesses to hire and support employees with disabilities. We work with employers to fill existing job openings—or can also help businesses to create customized positions to address small needs or support key staff. WorkLink has access to a wide range of job seekers—from entry level candidates to seasoned professionals. All candidates go through an in-depth evaluation process. Once hired, an on-site employment specialist is available to help situate the new employee. They develop training strategies and work closely with your staff to learn to effectively supervise and direct the new employee. It is a team-based approach to employment.
