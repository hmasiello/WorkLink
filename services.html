Consultation

WorkLink assesses your company's organizational needs. A WorkLink employment consultant meets with you to pinpoint staffing needs and potential job duties. WorkLink then recruits an applicant who matches your requirements. We draw from a pool of job seekers who are interested in both full and part time positions.



Recruitment

We work with businesses to recruit and train employees to fill a variety of professional, semiskilled and entry level positions. We can provide prescreened, highly motivated, dependable applicants to fill your staffing needs.



On-Site Assistance

WorkLink's employment consultant works with you and your staff to develop training and support strategies to ensure your new employee is able to do his or her job successfully.


On-site assistance may include situating the employee, developing skills, organizing tasks and locating accommodations or adaptive devices.


The length of our on-site training and support is determined by the needs of your business and the new employee.



Volunteer/Internship Programs

We aim to provide qualified and skilled applicants. As part of the assessment and evaluation process, we may develop an internship or volunteer opportunity in order to develop skills or explore a potential career path.
